Technical Issues

  • Conveyor not Working

    Please verify if the Motor is working or not, if yes, please check if there is any presence of dirt or mud particles under the rollers or belt sliding.

    Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.

  • Belt not Moving

    Please verify if the Motor is working or not, if yes, please check if there is any presence of dirt or mud particles under the rollers or belt sliding.

    Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.

  • Motor Not Working

    Please verify if your power supply is active, or if your battery is properly charged.

    If not, please charge your battery again and test again, if still not resolved please visit the nearby Service Centre.

    Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.

  • Belt is Slipping

    Please verify if the conveyor is mounted with limited load, make sure no excessive load is mounted.

    If still the problem is occurring, inspect the tightening of coupling of motor to roller.

    Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.

  • Belt moving with Drag

    Please check the following:

    1. Battery is properly charged.

    2. No excessive load is present on the belt.

    3. Rollers and bearings are properly lubricated.

    4. Belt is properly tensioned.

    Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.

  • Excessive Vibrations

    Please verify the following:

    1. Presence of damage or shearing on conveyor frame, if yes immediately contact the nearest service station.

    2. There is no excessive load mounted unevenly on the conveyor frame.

    Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.

  • Robotic Arm Not Working
    • Gripper is not Working

      Please Verify the Followings:

      1. All the wires are Properly connected.

      2. Gripper is not damaged.

      3. Battery is properly charged.

      4. Motors are working properly.

      Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.

    • Gripper is Damaged

      Please Verify the Followings:

      1. If the motors and wires are also damaged.

      2. If damaged component is not available in Spare kit.

      Either Contact with us by visiting contact option or else find nearby Service Centre.
